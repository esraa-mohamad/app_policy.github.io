## Description of Service

YUB Steam Worker is a mobile application for authorized field workers who fulfill car wash bookings placed by customers through the **YUB Steam Client** app. The App provides:

- Secure login with phone number and password (credentials issued by your administrator)
- View assigned orders with customer, vehicle, address, and package details
- Update order status through the service workflow (pending, arriving, in progress, completed)
- Upload before and after wash photos (up to four images per side per order)
- Schedule and calendar views to manage daily assignments
- Worker location updates for operational coordination
- Push notifications for new and updated orders
- Profile access and logout
- Complaints submission and access to privacy policy and terms

Workers **cannot** create customer bookings or register new worker accounts inside this App.
