## Description of Service

YUB Steam Client is a mobile application that allows customers to book professional car wash services at their chosen location. The App provides:

- User registration and profile management (individual or company accounts)
- Phone-based authentication with OTP verification
- Vehicle (car) management with photos and category-based pricing
- Saved addresses and map-based location selection
- Browse and book car wash packages and additional services
- Schedule visits, reschedule, or cancel booking times
- Apply discount coupons and use the points/rewards system
- Track active and completed orders, view invoices, and rate completed services
- Push notifications for booking updates
- Technical support and complaints submission

Field workers fulfill bookings through the separate **YUB Steam Worker** app. Workers do not create bookings on your behalf through the Client App.
