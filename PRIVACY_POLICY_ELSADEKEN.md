## 2. Permissions We Request

### 2.1 Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To allow you to take photos for your profile picture or share images in chats

### 2.2 Photo Library/Gallery Permission

- **Access:** We only access the specific photos you select, not your entire photo library
- **Control:** You can revoke photo access at any time through your device settings

### 2.3 Notification Permission
- **Purpose:** To send you important updates, messages, and app notifications
- **Types of Notifications:**
  - New message alerts
  - Profile view notifications
  - Match notifications
  - System updates and announcements
- **Control:** You can enable/disable notifications at any time through:
  - App settings
  - Device notification settings
- **Data Collection:** We collect notification interaction data (opened, dismissed) to improve our service

### 2.4 Internet/Network Permission
- **Purpose:** To connect to our servers and enable app functionality
- **Usage:** Required for all app features including messaging, profile updates, and content loading
- **Data Transfer:** All data is transmitted securely using encryption

### 2.5 Storage Permission
- **Purpose:** To cache data and store downloaded content locally
- **Usage:** Improves app performance and enables offline viewing of certain content
- **Control:** You can clear cached data through app settings

## 16. Specific Permission Details

### 16.1 Camera Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)
- **When Requested:** When you tap to take a photo
- **What We Access:** Camera hardware only
- **What We Don't Do:** We don't access your camera without your explicit action
- **How to Revoke:** Settings > Apps > Elsadeken > Permissions > Camera

### 16.2 Read External Storage (android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
- **When Requested:** When you select a photo from gallery
- **What We Access:** Only the specific photo you select
- **What We Don't Do:** We don't scan or access your entire photo library
- **How to Revoke:** Settings > Apps > Elsadeken > Permissions > Storage

### 16.3 Internet Permission (android.permission.INTERNET)
- **Purpose:** Required for all app functionality
- **What We Do:** Connect to our servers securely
- **Security:** All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption

### 16.4 Notification Permission (android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
- **When Requested:** On first app launch or when enabling notifications
- **What We Send:** Message alerts, activity updates, system notifications
- **How to Manage:** Settings > Apps > Elsadeken > Notifications
- **Frequency Control:** You can control notification frequency in app settings
