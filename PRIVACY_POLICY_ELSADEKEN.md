# Privacy Policy for Elsadeken

**Last Updated:** December 13, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Elsadeken. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our mobile application and tell you about your privacy rights.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and profile information
- **Authentication Data:** Login credentials and authentication tokens
- **Profile Data:** Profile pictures, bio, interests, and preferences
- **Location Data:** Country, city, and region information (if provided)

### 1.2 Usage Information
- **App Activity:** Features you use, actions you take, and time spent on the app
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Log Data:** IP address, browser type, access times, and pages viewed

### 1.3 Communication Data
- **Messages:** Chat messages, conversations, and communication history
- **Notifications:** Notification preferences and interaction data

## 2. Permissions We Request

### 2.1 Camera Permission
- **Purpose:** To allow you to take photos for your profile picture or share images in chats
- **Usage:** Only accessed when you explicitly choose to take a photo
- **Storage:** Photos are stored securely on our servers only if you choose to upload them
- **Control:** You can revoke camera access at any time through your device settings

### 2.2 Photo Library/Gallery Permission
- **Purpose:** To allow you to select existing photos from your device for profile pictures or sharing
- **Usage:** Only accessed when you choose to select a photo from your gallery
- **Access:** We only access the specific photos you select, not your entire photo library
- **Control:** You can revoke photo access at any time through your device settings

### 2.3 Notification Permission
- **Purpose:** To send you important updates, messages, and app notifications
- **Types of Notifications:**
  - New message alerts
  - Profile view notifications
  - Match notifications
  - System updates and announcements
- **Control:** You can enable/disable notifications at any time through:
  - App settings
  - Device notification settings
- **Data Collection:** We collect notification interaction data (opened, dismissed) to improve our service

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### 3.1 Service Provision
- Creating and managing your account
- Enabling communication between users
- Personalizing your experience
- Matching you with compatible users

### 3.2 Communication
- Sending you notifications about app activity
- Responding to your inquiries and support requests
- Sending important service updates

### 3.3 Improvement and Analytics
- Analyzing app usage to improve features
- Identifying and fixing technical issues
- Developing new features and services

### 3.4 Security and Safety
- Detecting and preventing fraud and abuse
- Enforcing our terms of service
- Protecting user safety and security

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We DO NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

### 4.2 We may share your information with:
- **Other Users:** Profile information you choose to make visible
- **Service Providers:** Third-party companies that help us operate our service (hosting, analytics, customer support)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Authentication:** Industry-standard authentication protocols
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
- **Regular Security Audits:** Ongoing monitoring and security assessments

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is deleted هmmediately

## 7. Children's Privacy

Elsadeken is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

## 8. Push Notifications

### 8.1 Types of Notifications
- **Message Notifications:** Alerts for new messages
- **Activity Notifications:** Profile views, likes, and matches
- **System Notifications:** Important updates and announcements

### 8.2 Managing Notifications
You can control notifications through:
- **In-App Settings:** Enable/disable specific notification types
- **Device Settings:** Manage all app notifications
- **Do Not Disturb:** Temporary notification muting

### 8.3 Notification Data
We collect data about notification delivery and interaction to improve our service.

## 9. Camera and Photo Usage

### 9.1 Photo Capture
- Camera access is only used when you explicitly choose to take a photo
- Photos are not automatically uploaded or shared
- You have full control over which photos to upload

### 9.2 Photo Storage
- Uploaded photos are stored securely on our servers
- Photos are only visible according to your privacy settings
- You can delete photos at any time

### 9.3 Photo Processing
- We may resize or compress photos for optimal performance
- No facial recognition or biometric data is collected
- Photos are not used for advertising or marketing purposes
