# Privacy Policy for YUB Steam Client App

**Last Updated:** May 16, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to YUB Steam ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the YUB Steam **Client** mobile application ("App") and related services at `app.yubsteam.com`.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

- **Account Registration:** Full name, phone number, country code, account type (individual or company), company name, commercial registration, tax number, and documents you upload for company accounts where applicable
- **Authentication:** OTP verification data and session tokens (we do not use password-based login for customers)
- **Profile Information:** Name, phone, and profile details you edit in the App
- **Vehicles:** Car brand, model, category, color, plate number, and photos you upload
- **Addresses:** Address labels, details, latitude and longitude from map selection
- **Bookings:** Selected packages, scheduled dates and times, notes, coupon codes, pricing, and order history
- **Ratings:** Star ratings and feedback for completed services
- **Support:** Technical support and complaint submissions, including subject, message text, and attached images
- **Payment Information:** Transaction details processed through secure third-party payment gateways (we do not store full payment card numbers on our servers)

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information, including:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and app version
- **Location Information:** GPS coordinates when you use the map to select or confirm a service address
- **Usage Data:** App features accessed, interaction data, and crash or performance logs
- **Log Data:** IP address, access times, and API request metadata
- **Push Notification Tokens:** Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens to deliver booking and service notifications

### Information from Third-Party Services

We may receive information from third-party services integrated with our App:

- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication-related tokens, cloud messaging, and notification delivery
- **Google Maps:** Map display, geocoding, and location coordinates for address selection
- **Payment Processors:** Payment confirmation and transaction status (where applicable)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Service Provision

- To create and manage your customer account
- To verify your phone number via OTP
- To store and manage your vehicles and saved addresses
- To process, schedule, modify, and cancel car wash bookings
- To apply coupons, calculate category-based pricing, and manage points/offers
- To generate invoices and order history
- To assign and coordinate fulfillment with field workers
- To display order status and before/after wash photos when uploaded by workers

### Location Services

- To let you pick and save service locations on a map
- To share the service address with assigned workers so they can perform the wash

### Communication

- To send push notifications about bookings, reminders, and service updates
- To respond to technical support and complaint requests
- To send important notices about our services or policy changes

### Analytics and Improvement

- To analyze usage patterns and improve App functionality
- To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues

### Legal Compliance

- To comply with applicable laws and regulations
- To respond to legal requests and protect our rights
- To enforce our Terms and Conditions

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

### Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

- **Firebase (Google):** Push notifications and app infrastructure
- **Google Maps:** Location and mapping services
- **Payment Processors:** Payment processing where applicable

### Field Workers and Operations

To fulfill your booking, we share necessary information with assigned workers and our operations team, including:

- Your name and phone number

Workers access this information only through the YUB Steam Worker app for order fulfillment.

### Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction with appropriate safeguards.

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

Your data is stored on secure servers connected to `app.yubsteam.com` and may be processed in countries other than your country of residence. We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- Encryption of sensitive data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure token-based authentication after OTP verification
- Access controls on server systems
- Regular security updates

### Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account through the App, subject to verification and legal retention requirements.
